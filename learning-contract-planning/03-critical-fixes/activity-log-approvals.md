For each undertaking, I will seek Team Company Director input on:

2. **Development Priority:** Is this the most valuable use of my time?
3. **Practical Feasibility:** Can this realistically be achieved given business demands?
4. **Resource Requirements:** What support or resources might I need?
5. **Success Metrics:** How will we measure learning and business outcomes?
