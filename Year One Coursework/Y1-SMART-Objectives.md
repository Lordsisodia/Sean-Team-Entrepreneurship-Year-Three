## Objective 1: Gym Consistency & Diet

**Duration:** Medium-term | **Type:** Self/Projects/Ventures | **Competency:** Gym Consistency

### SMART Framework

**Specific:** Attend the gym for specific workouts targeting muscle growth and achieve a balanced diet

**Measurable:**
- Attend gym 5 times per week
- Gain 1 kilogram each month
- Consume 3000 calories daily
- Target: 65kg by end of 2024

**Achievable:**
- Acquire fitness trainer
- Use diet-tracking app
- Set gym schedules

**Realistic:** Ensure work/personal commitments scheduled around gym times

**Time-bound:** Evaluate progress at end of each month

### Competency Integration

| Competency | Application |
|------------|-------------|
| **Strategic Planning & Visioning** | Draft monthly workout and meal roadmap; visualize long-term health outcomes |
| **Team Leadership & Management** | Collaborate with fitness trainer; engage in group gym sessions |
| **Digital Marketing & Branding** | Advocate for health on social media; connect with online health communities |
| **Negotiation & Persuasion** | Prioritize gym sessions; adjust timings as needed |
| **Data Analytics & Interpretation** | Utilize tracking apps to monitor calories and workouts; refine routines based on data |
| **Time Management & Productivity** | Schedule fixed times for gym and meal planning |

### Progress Report (April 25, 2024)

**Specific:**
- Gym Attendance: Averaging 4 times per week
- MMA Training: Ambition to recommence MMA training
- Dietary Routine: 2x 300g steaks, 6 eggs, ice cream, chocolate milk, protein shake
- Calorie Goal: Nearing 3000 calories (including 2L milk adding 1.3 cal)
- Home Training: 50 pull-ups, 200 push-ups, sit-ups daily
- Focus: Maintaining lean physique for optimal MMA performance

**Measurable:**
- Monthly assessment tracking gym attendance, muscle growth, dietary intake

**Achievable:**
- Utilizing fitness trainer guidance
- Using diet-tracking app
- Setting gym schedules around commitments

**Realistic:**
- Integrating gym sessions into daily routines
- Adjusting schedules as needed

**Time-bound:**
- Monthly assessments to review and adjust

**Level of Progress:** Intermediate

**Note:** While challenges in meeting all objectives consistently, progress being made. Adjustments in routines being implemented to maintain momentum toward long-term fitness objectives.

## Objective 2: Vaping Reduction

**Duration:** Short-term | **Type:** Self/Team/Projects | **Competency:** Vaping Reduction

### SMART Framework

**Specific:** Limit vaping sessions

**Measurable:**
- Reduce to 2 sessions per day
- Each lasting only a few minutes

**Achievable:**
- Gradually reduce frequency
- Use reminders/apps to track and motivate

**Realistic:**
- Address triggers leading to vaping
- Ensure alternatives in place

**Time-bound:** Achieve target in next 3 months

### Competency Integration

| Competency | Application |
|------------|-------------|
| **Strategic Planning & Visioning** | Design stepwise reduction plan; envision benefits of reduced vaping |
| **Team Leadership & Management** | Seek support group or friends for motivation and accountability |
| **Digital Marketing & Branding** | Engage with online communities focused on quitting vaping |
| **Negotiation & Persuasion** | During high cravings, remind of larger goal and benefits |
| **Data Analytics & Interpretation** | Track vaping frequency; adjust strategies based on feedback |
| **Time Management & Productivity** | Set specific vaping times; replace other sessions with productive activities |

### Progress Report (April 25, 2024)

**Specific:**
- Significant reduction achieved: 99% cut down on disposable vapes
- 95% general reduction
- Current: 3 vape sessions per day, lasting few minutes
- Strict no-cigarette rule
- Mentally repulsed by cigarettes
- Restricted nicotine levels in vapes

**Measurable:**
- Consistently limited to 3 sessions per day
- Gradually reducing reliance
- Using reminders and self-reflection

**Achievable:**
- Gradually reducing frequency over time
- Using reminders/apps
- Addressing triggers

**Realistic:**
- Transitioning to healthier alternatives (roll-ups)
- Ensuring alternatives in place

**Time-bound:**
- On track to achieve target within 3 months

**Level of Progress:** Advanced

**Note:** Significant progress made toward reducing vaping frequency. Transition away from disposable vapes and strict adherence to limits demonstrates commitment. Continued focus on addressing triggers and exploring healthier alternatives needed.

## Objective 5: Time Management

**Duration:** Medium-term | **Type:** Self | **Competency:** Time Management & Productivity

### SMART Framework

**Specific:** Enhance daily productivity

**Measurable:** Accomplish 80-100% of daily tasks consistently

**Achievable:**
- Use time management tools
- Set priorities
- Eliminate distractions

**Realistic:** Ensure tasks distributed evenly; avoid overloading

**Time-bound:** Consistently hit target for 3 consecutive months

### Competency Integration

| Competency | Application |
|------------|-------------|
| **Strategic Planning & Visioning** | Allocate time for high-priority projects; Eisenhower Box; clear weekly/monthly goals |
| **Team Leadership & Management** | Delegate tasks effectively; regular team progress reviews; share best practices |
| **Digital Marketing & Branding** | Planned content calendar; schedule posts using Buffer; monitor marketing time |
| **Negotiation & Persuasion** | Allocate time for negotiation prep; clear meeting agendas; assertive communication |
| **Data Analytics & Interpretation** | Track productivity metrics; use Toggl for time analytics; periodic review |
| **Time Management & Productivity** | Pomodoro Technique for breaks; Focus@Will to reduce distractions; Todoist/Trello for task organization |

### Progress Report (Since April 2020)

**Specific:**
- Task achievement ranges 50-100%
- Desire to stabilize at 80-100%
- 100% completion days result in pronounced sense of accomplishment

**Measurable:**
- Current: 50-100% task completion rate
- Target: Consistently 80-100%
- Higher completion leads to stronger sense of accomplishment

**Achievable:**
- Using time management tools
- Setting priorities
- Eliminating distractions

**Realistic:**
- Even task distribution
- Recognizing increased complexity of some tasks

**Time-bound:**
- Aim to consistently achieve 80-100% for 3 consecutive months

**Level of Progress:** Intermediate

**Note:** Progress made with task completion 50-100%. Challenges include task complexity and time allocation. Time management tools and prioritization strategies essential for improvement.
