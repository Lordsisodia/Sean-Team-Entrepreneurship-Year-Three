## LESSONS FOR FUTURE LC SECTIONS

### Successful Patterns to Replicate:

1. **Personal Experience Over Description:** Focus on YOUR experience, not external assessment results
2. **Team Apollo Specificity:** Use names, dates, specific situations
3. **Team Feedback Quotes:** Include direct quotes showing external validation
4. **Plans vs Reality:** Explicit comparison between what you planned and what actually happened
5. **Quantified Evidence:** Use specific numbers and metrics throughout
6. **Honest Self-Assessment:** Be MORE critical, not less
7. **Values Through Action:** Show values through specific decisions and trade-offs
8. **Applied Learning:** Demonstrate how you're currently applying what you've learned

### Patterns to Avoid:

1. ❌ Describing psychometric assessments (brief mention only)
2. ❌ Generic "problem-solving" without specific examples
3. ❌ Only strengths without honest weaknesses
4. ❌ Vague "team" without specific names
5. ❌ Description without critical evaluation
6. ❌ External focus (client savings, technical features) over internal learning
