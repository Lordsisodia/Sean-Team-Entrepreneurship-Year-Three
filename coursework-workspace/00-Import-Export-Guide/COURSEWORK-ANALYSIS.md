## üí° WHAT ARE UNDERTAKINGS?

**LfO Undertaking (Learning from Others):**
Each needs:
- **Activity:** What you did (book read, event attended, course taken, workshop participated in)
- **Critical Evaluation:** What you learned, how it changed your thinking, what surprised you
- **Synthesis:** How it connects to other knowledge/sources
- **Application:** How you applied this learning to your practice
- **Validity:** Is the source credible? Why?

**LbD Undertaking (Learning by Doing):**
Each needs:
- **Activity:** What you did (hands-on project, venture work, team challenge)
- **Experience:** What happened, what was challenging, what went well
- **Reflection:** What you learned from doing it
- **Application:** How you've used this learning since
- **Future:** How this will inform your future actions

**Competency Undertaking:**
Each needs:
- **Competency Name:** Which one from TE Framework
- **Current Level:** Where you started
- **Target Level:** Where you want to be
- **Activities:** What you've done to develop it (workshops, practice, reading, mentoring)
- **Progress:** Evidence of growth
- **Reflection:** What you've learned about this competency
