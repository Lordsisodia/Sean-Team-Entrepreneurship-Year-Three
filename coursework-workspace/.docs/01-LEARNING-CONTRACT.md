## üìã INDIVIDUAL QUESTION CRITERIA

### Q1: Where have I been? (~700 words)
- Specific Year 1 and Year 2 experiences
- Critical reflection on successes AND challenges
- Key learnings explicitly identified
- Honest self-assessment (privilege, mistakes)
- Patterns in behaviour recognised
- Connection to current situation

### Q2: Where am I now? (~700 words)
- Psychometric profile integrated (INTJ, multi-modal)
- Honest strengths AND weaknesses
- Current capabilities clearly articulated
- Self-awareness about privilege/responsibility
- Values and drivers explicitly stated
- Recognition of gaps

### Q3: Where do I want to get to? (~700 words)
- Clear, specific exit strategy
- Ambitious but achievable goals
- Connection to psychometric profile (Q2)
- Aligned to values and drivers
- Market context and validation
- Strategic thinking evident

### Q4: How will I get there? (~700 words)
- 6 competencies from TE Framework
- Current levels honestly assessed
- Target levels ambitious but achievable
- Each connects to exit strategy
- Specific actions for each
- Timeline established
- Mix of competency areas

### Q5: Where did I get to? (~700 words) - APRIL
- In-depth critical evaluation
- Key learning about habits/behaviours
- High self-awareness
- Personal accountability (no excuses)
- Plans vs reality analysis
- Critically informed judgment
- Significant future plans
