## ðŸŽ¯ Quick Reference: 90-100% Outstanding

### Learning Contract (Q1-Q5):
- All components complete
- Significant effort evident
- Detailed responses (not superficial)
- In-depth critical evaluation (not just description)
- High self-awareness and personal accountability
- Specific examples and honest reflection

### SMART Objectives:
- All 5 SMART criteria met
- Aligned to Learning Contract (Q3 exit strategy, Q4 competencies)
- Ambitious but achievable for Year 3
- Mix of short-term and medium-term
- Progress tracked and updated
- Feedback documented from 3 team reviews

### Activity Log:
- All 8 months complete (Sept-Apr)
- Minimum 2 LfO + 2 LbD activities per month
- Team approval each month
- Aligned to SMART Objectives
- Honest reflection (including challenges)
- Specific progress tracked

### Undertakings (18 total):
- All 18 undertakings complete (no missing)
- ~500-600 words each
- Critical evaluation (not just description)
- Specific application examples
- Honest self-reflection (includes failures)
- Connected to Learning Contract and objectives

## ðŸ“Š Grade Boundaries

| Grade | Range | Key Characteristic |
|-------|-------|-------------------|
| **Outstanding** | 90-100% | All complete + significant effort + in-depth critical evaluation |
| **Excellent** | 80-89% | Same as Outstanding (UWE doesn't distinguish) |
| **First Class** | 70-79% | All complete + detailed responses + good evaluation |
| **Upper Second** | 60-69% | Most complete + good effort but lacks depth |
| **Lower Second** | 50-59% | Basic completion, limited depth |
| **Third** | 40-49% | Significant gaps, superficial |
| **Fail** | 0-39% | Minimal engagement or incomplete |

**For 95% target:** You need Outstanding (90-100%) across all components.
