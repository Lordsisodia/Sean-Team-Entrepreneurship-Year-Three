## Purpose

Strategically expand SISO ecosystem through high-impact, mutually beneficial partnerships. Aligns with Learning Contract objectives: strengthen entrepreneurial competence, enhance strategic networking, build scalable business systems.

Supports SISO's team strategy of collaborative growth, enabling access to new markets, resources, and technological advancements. Contributes to exit strategy by establishing robust network and scalable partnership framework.
