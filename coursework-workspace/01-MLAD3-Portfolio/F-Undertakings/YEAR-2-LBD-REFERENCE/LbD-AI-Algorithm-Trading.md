## Purpose

Create AI-powered trading solution bridging algorithmic trading and user-friendly financial tools. Addresses market need for transparent, accessible, efficient trading automation.

Scalable, revenue-generating model with long-term potential. Contributes to exit strategy through sustainable profit-sharing system and replicable framework.

---

## Actions Undertaken

1. **Obtain and Translate Algorithms**
   - Partnered with traders for proven algorithms
   - Converted strategies into AI-compatible systems
   - Built on reliable, market-tested strategies

2. **Backtesting and Refinement**
   - Extensive backtesting with historical data
   - Iterative refinement based on results
   - Optimized for risk management and profitability

3. **Group Chat Deployment**
   - Launched free signal group chat
   - Collected feedback and monitored conversion
   - Trust-building mechanism before full automation

4. **Develop Client Interaction Systems**
   - Interface for paying clients: trade data, performance metrics, account details
   - Seamless processes for connecting accounts to AI trading bot
   - Enhanced user experience and operational scalability

5. **Continuous Strategy Improvement**
   - User feedback collection
   - Performance data tracking
   - Iterative refinement for enhanced outcomes
