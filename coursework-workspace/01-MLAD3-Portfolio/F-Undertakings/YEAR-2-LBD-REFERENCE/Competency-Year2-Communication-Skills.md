## Competency

**What is the name and definition of this competency?**

Communication Skills: The ability to convey ideas clearly and effectively, foster collaboration, and engage with others through both verbal and written communication.

**How is this competency relevant to my situation and SMART Objectives?**

Communication skills are critical for my objectives of developing and implementing a robust content strategy for the AI Influencer project and increasing active engagement within the team entrepreneurship community. Effective communication is essential for clearly articulating the value of our product to potential clients, crafting compelling content that resonates with our target audience, and ensuring that all team members are aligned with our strategic goals. This competency also supports my leadership role in mentoring new team members and enhancing collaboration within the community.

## Progression Summary

At the beginning of Year 2, I assessed my communication skills at an intermediate level. I was confident in my ability to communicate ideas, but I recognized that there was room for improvement, particularly in areas such as persuasive communication and content creation. Throughout the year, I worked on refining these skills, progressing to an advanced level by actively engaging in content development, public speaking, and team collaboration. My initial self-assessment was accurate, and my growth was facilitated by continuous practice and feedback from peers and mentors. The progression in this competency has been pivotal in achieving my SMART objectives, as it has enabled me to more effectively convey my vision and engage others in collaborative efforts.

## Key Learning

A significant learning moment in the development of my communication skills occurred during the creation of a content strategy for the AI Influencer project. As I crafted messaging to attract new clients, I realized the importance of tailoring communication to resonate with different audience segments. This experience taught me the value of empathy and audience awareness in crafting effective communication. By putting myself in the clients' shoes and considering their needs and pain points, I was able to create content that was not only informative but also persuasive and engaging.

Another key learning moment came during a series of team meetings where I had to present and advocate for new strategic initiatives. These presentations required me to clearly articulate complex ideas and persuade the team to align with the proposed direction. Through this process, I learned the importance of clarity, structure, and confidence in verbal communication. The positive feedback from the team and the successful adoption of the initiatives reinforced my understanding of how powerful effective communication can be in driving collective action.

## Progression through Practice

My progression in communication skills has been largely driven by hands-on practice in both content creation and team interactions. In developing the content strategy for the AI Influencer project, I regularly engaged in writing and editing content, which allowed me to refine my written communication skills. I also took on more public speaking opportunities, both within the team entrepreneurship community and at external events, which helped me build confidence and improve my verbal communication.

In addition, I made a conscious effort to enhance my active listening skills during team meetings and one-on-one interactions. By focusing on truly understanding others' perspectives before responding, I was able to foster more productive discussions and build stronger relationships within the team. This practice has not only improved my communication skills but also contributed to a more collaborative and inclusive team environment.

## Progression through Enquiry

Enquiry-based learning has played a significant role in developing my communication skills. I engaged with various resources, including books, online courses, and workshops focused on effective communication, storytelling, and content marketing. One particularly impactful resource was a course on persuasive communication, which provided practical techniques for influencing and engaging audiences. This course deepened my understanding of how to structure messages to achieve specific outcomes, whether in writing or speech.

I also participated in peer review sessions where we provided feedback on each other's presentations and written work. This process of giving and receiving constructive feedback helped me identify areas for improvement and reinforced the importance of clarity and conciseness in communication. Additionally, I studied successful communication strategies used by leading companies in the AI and tech industries, which provided valuable insights into best practices that I could apply to my own work.
