## 📝 COMPETENCY UNDERTAKING STRUCTURE

Each Competency Undertaking should follow this structure:

**Word Count:** ~500-600 words total

### Section 1: Competency Context (50-75 words)
- Which competency this addresses
- Current level vs. target level
- Why this competency matters for your exit strategy

### Section 2: Development Activities (100-125 words)
- Specific activities undertaken to develop this competency
- Resources used (mentorship, courses, reading, practice)
- Timeline of development efforts

### Section 3: Critical Evaluation (125-150 words)
- What insights did you gain about this competency?
- What challenged your previous assumptions?
- What surprised you about the development process?
- How did this change your understanding?

### Section 4: Application Evidence (125-150 words)
- Specific examples of applying this competency
- Impact on your ventures/projects/team
- Measurable outcomes or improvements
- Connection to SMART Objective progress

### Section 5: Validity & Reflection (75-100 words)
- How do you know you've developed?
- What evidence supports your claim?
- What gaps remain?
- What's next for continued development?

## 💡 OUTSTANDING QUALITY INDICATORS

For 90-100% (Outstanding) on Competency Undertakings:

**Content Quality:**
- [ ] Honest assessment of current level (not overstating)
- [ ] Specific development activities (not vague "I worked on it")
- [ ] Critical evaluation (not just description)
- [ ] Concrete application examples with measurable impact
- [ ] Valid evidence of development (not just "I feel better")

**Depth of Reflection:**
- [ ] Connections to Learning Contract (Q4)
- [ ] Links to SMART Objectives
- [ ] Integration of theory/frameworks
- [ ] Awareness of limitations and remaining gaps
- [ ] Clear next steps for continued development

**Writing Quality:**
- [ ] Clear, professional language
- [ ] Appropriate word count (~500-600 each)
- [ ] Logical structure and flow
- [ ] Specific examples and details
- [ ] No fluff or filler
