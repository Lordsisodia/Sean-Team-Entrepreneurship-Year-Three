## 📝 PEBBLEPAD STRUCTURE (Each Undertaking)

### **Section 1: Competency** (~150-200 words)
- **What is the name and definition of this competency?**
- **How is this competency relevant to my situation and SMART Objectives?**

### **Section 2: Progression Summary** (~250-300 words)
- Starting level (Year 2 baseline)
- Year 3 level reached
- Was this in line with expectations?
- Accuracy of initial self-evaluation
- Were you ambitious enough?
- Key factors that contributed to progression

### **Section 3: Key Learning** (~400-500 words)
- 2-3 significant learning moments
- What you discovered/realized
- How this changed your understanding
- Specific examples with dates

### **Section 4: Progression through Practice** (~400-500 words)
- Specific projects/ventures/activities
- How you put competency into practice
- Timeline (when things happened)
- People involved
- Measurable outcomes
- Impact on overall development

### **Section 5: Progression through Enquiry** (~400-500 words)
- Theories/models/frameworks learned
- Key insights from different sources
- Comparing/contrasting perspectives
- How values/ethics influenced you
- How knowledge enabled more effective practice

### **Section 6: Next Steps** (~200-300 words)
- How to continue developing
- Specific next milestones
- Application to projects/ventures
- Professional/self development
- Long-term post-TE goals

**Total Word Count per Undertaking:** ~1,800-2,200 words
