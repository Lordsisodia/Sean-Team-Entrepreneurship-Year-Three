## Competency

**What is the name and definition of this competency?**

Project Management: The ability to plan, organize, and manage resources effectively to see a project through to successful completion.

**How is this competency relevant to my situation and SMART Objectives?**

Project management is crucial to achieving my objectives of expanding the AI Influencer project from 6 to over 100 influencers, implementing a robust content strategy, and streamlining internal processes through automation. Effective project management will enable me to coordinate multiple tasks, allocate resources efficiently, and ensure that all initiatives are completed on time and within budget. This competency is also essential for managing the team, ensuring that all members are aligned with project goals, and maintaining high standards throughout the project lifecycle.

---

## Progression Summary

At the start of Year 2, I rated my project management skills as intermediate. I had experience managing smaller projects, but the scale and complexity of the AI Influencer expansion required a more advanced level of expertise. Over the year, I focused on improving my planning, organization, and resource management skills, progressing to an advanced level. This was achieved by applying structured project management methodologies and tools, such as Gantt charts and project management software, to track progress and manage dependencies. My initial self-assessment proved to be a realistic starting point, and the ambitious goals I set have pushed me to develop this competency further. Key factors contributing to my progression include consistent practice, learning from past project outcomes, and seeking feedback from mentors.

---

## Key Learning

A key learning moment in my development of project management skills occurred during the initial planning phase of the AI Influencer expansion. Faced with the challenge of scaling from 6 to over 100 influencers, I realized the importance of detailed planning and risk management. I learned that anticipating potential obstacles and creating contingency plans were essential for keeping the project on track. This experience taught me the value of proactive management and the need to constantly monitor progress against milestones to ensure timely delivery.

Another significant learning moment came during the process of streamlining internal operations through automation. This involved coordinating the efforts of multiple team members, setting clear objectives, and ensuring that everyone adhered to the established timelines. I discovered the importance of clear communication and delegation in project management, as well as the need for regular check-ins to address any issues early on. The successful reduction of manual workload by 50% by the end of the project was a testament to the effectiveness of these strategies.

---

## Progression through Practice

I achieved progression in project management through hands-on involvement in various projects, particularly the AI Influencer expansion and the internal process automation initiatives. In managing these projects, I applied project management principles such as setting SMART goals, creating detailed project plans, and regularly reviewing progress against these plans. I also used project management tools to track tasks, manage deadlines, and allocate resources efficiently.

One of the most impactful practices was implementing a regular review process where I and my team would assess project progress, identify bottlenecks, and adjust plans as needed. This practice not only improved project outcomes but also enhanced my ability to manage complex projects involving multiple stakeholders and interdependencies. Additionally, I took on the role of mentor within the team, guiding newer members in project management practices and helping them develop their skills.

---

## Progression through Enquiry

Enquiry-based learning played a significant role in deepening my understanding of project management. I engaged with a variety of resources, including books on project management methodologies like Agile and Waterfall, as well as online courses on advanced project management techniques. One particularly valuable resource was a course on Agile project management, which provided insights into managing projects in dynamic environments where requirements might change rapidly. This course helped me adapt my project management approach to be more flexible and responsive to changes.

I also participated in workshops and seminars focused on project management, where I had the opportunity to discuss challenges and best practices with other entrepreneurs and project managers. These discussions broadened my perspective and provided practical tips that I could apply directly to my projects. Furthermore, I studied case studies of successful project management in the tech industry, which offered real-world examples of how to overcome common project challenges.

---

## Next Steps

Moving forward, I plan to continue developing my project management skills by exploring more advanced project management methodologies, such as Lean project management, which focuses on maximizing value while minimizing waste. I will also seek opportunities to manage larger, more complex projects that challenge my current skill level and push me to innovate in my approach. Additionally, I intend to continue mentoring others in project management, as teaching these skills reinforces my own learning and contributes to the development of the team. By continually refining this competency, I aim to enhance my ability to manage large-scale projects efficiently, ensuring their successful completion and contributing to the growth and success of my ventures.
