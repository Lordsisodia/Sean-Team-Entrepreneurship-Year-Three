## PEBBLEPAD FORM STRUCTURE (All 6 Competencies)

Each competency undertaking uses the exact same 6-section structure:

### Section 1: Competency (Max 200 words)
- Competency name and definition
- Relevance to Year 3 situation and SMART objectives
- Why critical for your exit strategy

### Section 2: Progression Summary (Max 300 words)
- Year 2 starting level
- Year 3 level reached
- Was progression in line with expectations?
- Accuracy of initial self-evaluation
- Were you ambitious enough?
- Key factors that contributed to progression

### Section 3: Key Learning (Max 500 words)
- 2-3 significant learning moments
- What you discovered/realized
- How this changed your understanding
- Specific examples with dates

### Section 4: Progression through Practice (Max 500 words)
- Specific projects/ventures/activities
- How you put competency into practice
- Timeline (when things happened)
- People involved
- Measurable outcomes

### Section 5: Progression through Enquiry (Max 500 words)
- Theories/models/frameworks learned
- Key insights from different sources
- Comparing contrasting perspectives
- How values/ethics influenced you
- How knowledge enabled more effective practice

### Section 6: Next Steps (Max 300 words)
- How to continue developing
- Specific next milestones
- Application to projects/ventures
- Professional/self development
- Long-term post-TE goals

**Total Word Count per Undertaking:** ~1,800-2,200 words

## WRITING TIPS FOR EACH SECTION

### Section 1 (Competency): Keep it Focused
- State definition clearly
- Connect to specific SMART objective
- Mention 2-3 key evidence points
- Target: 150-200 words

### Section 2 (Progression Summary): Be Honest
- Start with Year 2 level from saved competency
- Was progression expected? (yes/no/why)
- Was initial assessment accurate? (be honest)
- Were you ambitious enough? (be honest)
- List 3-4 specific progression factors
- Target: 250-300 words

### Section 3 (Key Learning): Use Stories
- 2-3 specific moments with dates
- For each: What happened? What did you learn? How did it change you?
- Connect moments to competency development
- Target: 450-500 words

### Section 4 (Practice): Be Specific
- 3-4 specific practices/projects
- For each: Timeline? People involved? What did you do? What was the outcome?
- Show how practice developed competency
- Target: 450-500 words

### Section 5 (Enquiry): Connect Theory to Practice
- 3-4 frameworks/theories learned
- For each: Source? Key insights? How applied? What was the impact?
- Discuss values/ethics influence
- Show how knowledge enabled better practice
- Target: 450-500 words

### Section 6 (Next Steps): Be Concrete
- 2-3 specific milestones with timelines
- How will you apply this going forward?
- Long-term post-TE goals
- Acknowledge remaining gaps
- Target: 200-250 words
