## Overview

## Key Elements

### 1. Specific Action

- **SaaS Metrics:** Studied MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue), ARR (Annual Run Rate), churn rate, net revenue retention
- **Unit Economics:** Analyzed contribution margin, customer acquisition cost, lifetime value
- **LTV:CAC Research:** Studied ratios (3:1 healthy, 5:1 excellent), payback periods (12-month ideal)
- **Financial Patterns:** Researched subscription business models (usage-based pricing, tiered pricing, expansion revenue)
- **Benchmark Analysis:** Compared SISO metrics to industry standards (Bessemer, KeyBanc reports)

### 2. Purpose & Importance

- **Financial Literacy:** Understanding SaaS business model economics (partnership program is SaaS-like)
- **Business Health:** Learning how to measure and optimize financial performance
- **Investor Readiness:** Knowing metrics that VCs and acquirers care about

### 3. Connection to Competency

- **Primary:** Financial Management & Unit Economics (Project)

- Shows strategic thinking: understanding economics before scaling

### 4. Connection to Objective

- **Objective 6:** Partnership App + 100 Partnerships - financial metrics inform partnership program economics
- Applied to partnership commission structure, revenue projections

### 5. Impact (Evidence)

- **Financial Dashboard:** Built revenue dashboard in December (P25) using metrics learned in this study
- **Pricing Strategy:** Applied unit economics to partnership commission structure (15-30% based on LTV calculations)
- **Business Health:** Started tracking MRR, churn, NRR (November baseline)
