## Overview

**What I Researched:**
Benchmarked 8 major partnership platforms (PartnerStack, Impact.com, Post Affiliate Pro, Tapfiliate, ReferralRock, Ambassador, GrowthHero, Rewardful), analyzed pricing models and revenue structures across competitors, documented onboarding flows and user experiences, and identified feature gaps and differentiation opportunities.

---

## Key Elements

### 1. Specific Action

- **Hands-on Analysis:** Created trial accounts on 8 platforms, experienced onboarding flows firsthand
- **Pricing Analysis:** Documented commission structures, platform fees, tiered pricing, revenue models
- **Feature Comparison:** Created 50-point feature comparison matrix (dashboard, reporting, automation, integrations)
- **UX Evaluation:** Screen-recorded onboarding flows, analyzed friction points, identified best practices
- **Differentiation:** Identified 3 gaps in competitor offerings (white-label tools, agency-specific features, automated outreach)

### 2. Purpose & Importance

- **Competitive Intelligence:** Understanding what existing platforms offer (avoid reinventing wheel)
- **Differentiation Strategy:** Finding gaps where SISO can compete (not just copying)
- **Pricing Strategy:** Learning how successful platforms price and monetize

### 3. Connection to Competency

- **Primary:** Strategic Business Analysis & Market Positioning (Project)
- Demonstrates analytical rigor: systematic comparison across 8 competitors
- Shows strategic thinking: differentiation vs. me-too approach

### 4. Connection to Objective

- **Objective 6:** Partnership App + 100 Partnerships - this research directly informed Partnership App architecture (P7)
- Competitive insights shaped partnership program value proposition (white-label SISO tools as differentiator)

### 5. Impact (Evidence)

- **Strategic:** Identified 3 differentiation opportunities (white-label tools, agency focus, automation) - all implemented in Partnership App
- **Pricing:** Adopted tiered commission structure (15-30%) based on competitor analysis
- **Feature Prioritization:** Avoided building features competitors already had (focused on differentiation)
- **Validation:** Competitor analysis confirmed market gap - no platform offered white-label agency tools
- **Long-term:** These insights shaped partnership program positioning in all outreach (December-January)

1. **PartnerStack** - Enterprise partnership platform

3. **Automated Outreach** - Missing automated partnership communication tools

### Strategic Applications
- Partnership App architecture shaped by competitive insights
- Tiered commission structure (15-30%) based on market analysis
- Feature prioritization focused on differentiation
- Partnership program positioning emphasized unique value props
