## 💡 Rationale

### Why This Objective?
**Scaling Requirement:** Objective 6 (100 partnerships) requires closing 6 partnerships/week. Manual outreach doesn't scale. Automation is mandatory.

**Team Leverage:** Team Apollo provides distribution network but requires systems to ensure quality and consistency at scale.

**Service Design Competency:** Designing end-to-end outreach process (scripts → workflows → training → analytics) is pure Service Design work.

### Why This Approach?
**Commission Model:** 20% of first-year revenue eliminates recruitment/salary costs, making it infinitely scalable. Team Apollo only paid on success.

**In-App Training:** Embedding training documentation directly in the app ensures onboarding quality and reduces support burden.

**60 Conversations → 6 Partnerships:** 10% conversion is conservative but realistic for qualified leads. 60 conversations/week is achievable (12/day × 5 days).

### Why This Timeline?
**February 28 Deadline:** Provides 1-month buffer before March outreach launch. Allows for testing and iteration.

**March Launch:** Aligns with final push for Objective 6 (100 partnerships by April 30). 8-week run (March + April) provides sufficient runway.

**5-Month Development:** Acknowledges complexity of building automated outreach system with training components.
