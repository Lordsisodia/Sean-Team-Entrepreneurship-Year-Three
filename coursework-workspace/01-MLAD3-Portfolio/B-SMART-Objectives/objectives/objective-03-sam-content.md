## 💡 Rationale

### Why This Objective?
**Delegation Challenge:** Year 2 feedback identified difficulty letting go of control and trusting others to execute. This objective forces delegation practice with a low-stakes, high-learning environment.

**Pipeline Validation:** Before launching full Team Apollo commission outreach (Objective 4), validating content-driven lead generation with Sam provides learnings and reduces risk.

**Strategic Leadership Competency:** Leading others, setting strategy, providing creative direction - all core Strategic Leadership behaviors requiring development.

### Why This Approach?
**3 Posts/Week:** Manageable cadence that allows quality without overwhelming Sam's capacity. Provides sufficient data for lead generation testing.

**Instagram + LinkedIn:** Both platforms relevant to agency partnership audience. Instagram for visual brand appeal, LinkedIn for B2B decision makers.

**100 Leads Target:** Approximately 1 lead per day over 4-month period, assuming 3 posts/week with conversion funnel. Mathematically sound but ambitious.

### Why This Timeline?
**4-Month Duration:** Sufficient runway to test, iterate, and validate content strategy before Objective 6 (100 partnerships) major push in March-April.

**Early Brief Deadline (Oct 20):** Gives Sam 2 weeks to prepare before November 1 start date, ensuring quality from day 1.

**Progressive Lead Targets:** 25 → 50 → 100 allows for early detection if approach isn't working, enabling pivot.
