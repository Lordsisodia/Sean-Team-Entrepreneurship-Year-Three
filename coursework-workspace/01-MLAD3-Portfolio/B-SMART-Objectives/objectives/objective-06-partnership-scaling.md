## 💡 Rationale

### Why This Objective?
**Exit Strategy:** This is the core exit strategy from my Learning Contract. If achieved, it validates the entire business model and provides a sustainable revenue foundation post-graduation.

**Entrepreneurial Judgment:** Making a strategic bet on partnership scaling vs. direct sales. If successful, leverages team for 100× output vs. individual effort.

**Financial Ambition:** £50K monthly revenue is meaningful business scale. Demonstrates ability to execute at high level.

### Why This Approach?
**Partnership Model vs. Direct Sales:** Direct sales to 1,000 end clients is impossible single-handedly. Partnership model leverages 100 agencies who each bring 10 clients. Force multiplier.

**Commission-Based Outreach:** Team Apollo earns 20% of first-year revenue. No salary cost means infinite scalability. Only pay for success.

**100 Agencies × 10 Clients:** Each agency only needs 10 clients to hit targets. Very achievable for agencies with existing client bases. Low barrier to partnership.

**£500/Agency Monthly:** At 2 clients × £250/month per client, agency gets white-label AI automation for their clients at profit margin. Win-win.

### Why This Timeline?
**December 31 Partnership App:** Gives 2 months after Client App completion (Nov 30) to build partnership-specific features (partner dashboard, commission tracking).

**April 30 Graduation:** Hard deadline. Aligns with academic year completion. Creates urgency.

**17-Week Outreach Window:** March 1 - April 30 = 17 weeks. At 6 partnerships/week = 102 partnerships. Math includes buffer for missed weeks.
