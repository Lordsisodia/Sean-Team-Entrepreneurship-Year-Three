## 💡 Rationale

### Why This Objective?
**Year 2 Learning Moment:** My most significant learning from Year 2 was the impact of environmental design on habit formation. Cannabis usage was identified as a primary blocker to achieving consistent productivity and achieving my potential.

**Academic Performance:** Substance use directly impacts my ability to achieve 70 focused hours/week, which is foundational to all other objectives.

**Self-Management Competency:** Developing self-discipline and habit management is a core Self-Management skill. This objective provides concrete evidence of this competency development.

### Why This Approach?
**100% Binary Metric:** Absolute clarity on success/failure. No gray areas.

**Daily Verification:** Nightly checkout creates accountability ritual and prevents "drift" where small compromises accumulate.

**Written Justification for Lapses:** Prevents rationalization. Any slip must be documented and justified, creating friction that discourages lapses.

**Shared Tracking with Objective 1:** Both objectives use the same time-boxing app, creating synergy. Tracking focused hours automatically tracks cannabis-free compliance.

### Why This Timeline?
**7-Month Duration:** Covers entire academic year through graduation, ensuring sustained performance during critical period.

**Graduated Hours Target:** 50 hours minimum (achievable) with 70 hours stretch (aspirational). Acknowledges that cannabis-free lifestyle will gradually increase capacity rather than instantly doubling productivity.

**Monthly Checkpoints:** Regular review prevents drift and allows for adjustment if needed.
