## 💡 Rationale

### Why This Objective?
**Critical Path Dependency:** Objective 6 (100 partnerships) is impossible without a proven client app. Agencies won't partner without a working product their clients can use.

**Risk Mitigation:** Launching 100 partnerships with an untested client app would be catastrophic. Testing with 5-10 clients first de-risks the entire business model.

**Innovation Management Competency:** Building novel AI automation product from scratch demonstrates Innovation Management (concept → build → test → iterate).

### Why This Approach?
**Free MVP Trial:** Removes pricing friction, increases conversion rate for test clients. Creates goodwill and detailed feedback.

**100% Satisfaction Target:** Ambitious but necessary. Anything below 95% suggests product-market fit issues. Forcing 100% creates accountability to address every client concern.

**3 Feedback Iterations:** Agile approach. Build → Measure → Learn cycle repeated 3 times ensures product quality before scaling.

### Why This Timeline?
**November 30 MVP:** 8 weeks from now gives sufficient runway for 60% → 100% completion without rushing.

**December Client Onboarding:** Month-long onboarding window (Dec 1-31) allows for careful client selection and setup.

**January Satisfaction Target:** 3 full weeks of client usage (Jan 1-21) + 1 week for final survey (Jan 22-31) provides robust data.
