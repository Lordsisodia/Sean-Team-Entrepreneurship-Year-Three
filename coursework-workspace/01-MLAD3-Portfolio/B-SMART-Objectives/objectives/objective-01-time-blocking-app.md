## 💡 Rationale

### Why This Objective?
Time management is the foundational challenge identified in my Learning Contract (Q2: Where Am I Now?). Without a robust system for tracking and maximizing focused work hours, scaling to achieve the other 5 objectives is impossible. This objective directly addresses the "time management" weakness identified in Year 2 feedback.

### Why This Approach?
**Time-blocking over task-counting:** Previous Year 2 attempts used task completion metrics, which proved flawed (easy to game, low correlation with actual productivity). Hours logged is a superior metric because it reflects actual work capacity.

**Automated tracking:** Manual time tracking failed in Year 2 due to compliance issues. Automation removes the friction and ensures consistent data.

**Nightly checkout:** Provides accountability and creates a daily ritual for reflection on time usage.

### Why This Timeline?
**7-week ramp:** The progression from MVP launch to 70 hours/week assumes:
- Week 1-2: MVP deployment and bug fixes
- Week 3-4: Establish baseline (50 hours)
- Week 5-6: Optimize and increase (60 hours)
- Week 7: Target achievement (70 hours)

This gradual approach acknowledges the challenge of more than doubling productive capacity while ensuring sustainability.
