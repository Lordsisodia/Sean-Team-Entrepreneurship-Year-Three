### Critical Issue #3: Unverifiable Metrics

**Problem:** Claims that can't be substantiated.

**Examples:**
- "retention improved by 30%" - What retention? From what to what? Over what period?
- "cut manual workload by 50%" - Measured how? From X hours to Y hours?
- "enabling them to secure £50,000" - How are you enabling? Did they secure it?

**Rule:** If you can't answer the specific question, remove the claim.
