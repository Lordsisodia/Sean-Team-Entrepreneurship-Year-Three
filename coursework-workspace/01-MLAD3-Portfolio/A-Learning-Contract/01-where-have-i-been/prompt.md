## YEAR 2 FEEDBACK: THE CRITICAL PATTERNS TO AVOID

### Pattern 1: Technical Description vs Personal Experience 🔴 MOST CRITICAL

**Year 2 Issue (53% grade):**
> "They predominantly focus on technical learning related to your ventures, with little contextual evidence of your actual experience."

**The Fix:**
- ❌ DON'T: "I developed a 75-step automation system that processes PDR research..."
- ✅ DO: "Building the automation system taught me that I thrive on systematic problem-solving, but I also struggle with perfectionism—I spent 6 weeks refining the system while partnership apps remained incomplete..."

**Test for Every Sentence:**
**"Is this sentence about ME and MY experience?"**

If the sentence is about:
- A technical system you built → Rewrite to focus on what you learned
- A project you completed → Rewrite to focus on how it changed you
- A skill you developed → Rewrite to focus on the personal journey

### Pattern 2: Generic vs Team Apollo Specific 🔴

**Year 2 Issue:**
> "Each of these could also be related to your experiences with Team Apollo, to strengthen the evidence in being more specific."

**The Fix:**
- ❌ DON'T: "I developed communication skills through various interactions."
- ✅ DO: "When working with Sam on the agency partnership model in September 2024, I struggled to explain technical concepts clearly, which resulted in a 25% closing rate."

**Required Elements:**
- Specific team member names (Sam, Pranav, etc.)
- Specific projects (agency model, crypto client)
- Specific dates/timeframes
- Specific outcomes (numbers, results)

### Pattern 3: All AI Focus 🔴

**Year 2 Enquiry Issue (40% grade):**
> "All the LfO evidence is very focused on your AI venture elements, and lack thorough and meaningful evaluation of professional values, ethics, practices and behaviours."

**The Fix:**
- Diversify beyond AI/automation
- Include: leadership, teamwork, ethics, values, communication, behaviours

### Pattern 4: Not Critical Enough (Too Positive) 🟡

**Year 2 Self-Leadership Feedback (62% - your best module):**
> "More critical evaluation of your own development would have added more depth."

**The Fix:**
- Be honest about failures
- Don't shy away from difficult truths
- Show self-awareness through honest assessment

This AI agent automatically executes context engineering for every client, transforming research from a 2-week manual process yielding 10% completeness to a 2-hour automated process achieving full comprehensive analysis."

When Sam asked in September why I was still optimizing instead of doing outreach, I felt defensive—my perfectionism exposed as avoidance behaviour. This pattern of hiding in technical work when faced with uncomfortable interpersonal tasks is something I'm actively working to change."

**Notice:**
- Technical achievement: Briefly mentioned (15 words)
- Personal insight emphasized (50+ words)
- Specific failure highlighted (6-week delay)
- Pattern identified (perfectionism as avoidance)
- Team member included (Sam)
- Specific date (September)
- Self-awareness about struggle
