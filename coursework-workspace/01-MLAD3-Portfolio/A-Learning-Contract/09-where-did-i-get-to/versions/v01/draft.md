## What This Section Will Include (When You Write It)

Based on the 90-100% Outstanding criteria, this section should contain:

1. **In-depth critical evaluation** of your Self-leadership activities
2. **Key learning about personal habits and behaviours**
3. **High levels of self-awareness** (honest, no defensiveness)
4. **Personal accountability** (owning mistakes, not blaming)
5. **Plans vs reality analysis** (what you planned vs actual)
6. **Critically informed judgment** (evidence-based conclusions)
7. **Significant future plans** (specific next steps)

## Notes to Collect Throughout the Year

To make writing this section easier, keep notes on:

### Significant Wins
- What went better than expected?
- What are you most proud of?
- What surprised you about your capabilities?

### Significant Failures
- What didn't go according to plan?
- What do you wish you'd done differently?
- What held you back?

### Patterns You Notice
- Habits that help you succeed
- Habits that hold you back
- Behaviours that repeat (positive or negative)
- Insights about yourself

### Evidence of Growth
- Specific examples of competency development
- Measurable progress on objectives
- Feedback from others
- Changes in your approach/behaviour
