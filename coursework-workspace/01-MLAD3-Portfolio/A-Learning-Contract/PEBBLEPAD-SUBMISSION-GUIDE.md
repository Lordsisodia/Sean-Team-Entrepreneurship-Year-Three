## Troubleshooting

**Issue: Word count differs in PebblePad**
- Solution: PebblePad may count differently. Focus on being within target ranges.

**Issue: Formatting lost**
- Solution: Re-apply bold/italics manually. Content is more important than formatting.

**Issue: Special characters appear**
- Solution: Replace em-dashes (—) with hyphens (-) if needed.

**Issue: Tables don't format correctly**
- Solution: Recreate table in PebblePad using their table tool.
