## Phase 2: Feedback Synthesis

### Critical Issues (Must Fix for 90%+)

**Issue 1: Description vs. Critical Evaluation**
- **Problem:** Content tends to describe WHAT happened rather than evaluating WHY it matters
- **Impact:** Prevents "in-depth critical evaluation" required for 90-100%
- **Fix Required:** Add analysis sentences starting with "This reveals that..." "This demonstrates..." "The significance is..."

**Issue 2: Pattern Identification Missing**
- **Problem:** Experiences treated as isolated events, not connected into behavioural patterns
- **Impact:** Misses "key learning about personal habits and behaviours"
- **Fix Required:** Add pattern statements: "Across X, Y, Z, I notice a consistent pattern where..."

**Issue 3: Values/Drivers Not Explicit**
- **Problem:** What motivates you is implied but not clearly stated
- **Impact:** Weakens connection to Q3 exit strategy
- **Fix Required:** Add explicit values statement: "My core values are X, Y, Z because..."

**Issue 4: Psychometric Integration Could Be Deeper**
- **Problem:** Assessment results listed but not deeply connected to actual behaviour
- **Impact:** Misses opportunity to show self-awareness
- **Fix Required:** For each trait, add: "This manifests in my work when I [specific situation]..."

**Issue 5: Failures Could Be More Honest**
- **Problem:** Focuses more on successes than failures
- **Impact:** Reduces "personal accountability" demonstration
- **Fix Required:** Add 1-2 specific Year 2 failures with what you learned
