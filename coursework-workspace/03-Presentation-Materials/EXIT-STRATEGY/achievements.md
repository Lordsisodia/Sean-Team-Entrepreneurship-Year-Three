## Business Model Transformation

### Before: Time-for-Money Model
Reliant on direct coding work - founder trades hours for pounds

### After: AI Infrastructure Model
- £1,000 setup fee
- £200/month retainer
- 10% operational costs
- Infrastructure does the work

**Significance for Exit Strategy:** This shift transforms the business from a service company (low valuation, founder-dependent) to a productised SaaS-like model (higher valuation, scalable). The infrastructure being the "worker" means:
- Lower operational costs
- Higher margins
- Not dependent on Shaan's direct involvement
- More predictable, recurring revenue

This is the key unlock for exit readiness.
