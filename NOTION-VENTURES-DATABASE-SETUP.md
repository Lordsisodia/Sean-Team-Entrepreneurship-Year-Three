# Notion Ventures & Projects Database Setup Guide

## Overview
This guide contains all 22 ventures from your coursework, formatted and ready to be added to your Notion workspace. Due to MCP server limitations, you'll need to create the database structure manually in Notion, then copy/paste the content below.

## Database Structure to Create

### Create a New Database Called: "Ventures & Projects Hub"

**Properties to Add:**
1. **Name** (Title)
2. **Category** (Select): AI & Automation, Client Project, Partnership, Skill Development, Strategic Management, E-commerce, Team Role
3. **Status** (Select): Ongoing, Completed, Pivoted, Terminated
4. **Year** (Select): Year 1, Year 2, Year 3
5. **Date Started** (Date)
6. **Date Ended** (Date)
7. **Role** (Text)
8. **Brief Description** (Text)
9. **Pre-Project Review** (Text)
10. **Interim Review** (Text)
11. **Post-Project Review** (Text)
12. **Key Learnings** (Text)
13. **Competency Development** (Multi-select): Service Design & Delivery, Strategic Leadership, Professional Communication, Self-Management, Financial Management, Entrepreneurial Judgment
14. **SMART Objective Link** (Text)
